## OBJECTION HANDLING

**"I'm too busy"**
→ "That's actually perfect — we're not looking for planners who need more work, we're looking for planners who want better-quality enquiries without doing any of the marketing themselves."

**"25% is too high"**
→ "The way to think about it is — we're delivering you a fully qualified, pre-vetted client who already wants to work with you. No advertising spend, no time wasted on bad-fit enquiries. A client you'd never have found otherwise."

**"I've tried lead sites before and the leads are terrible"**
→ "Completely understood — and that's exactly why we exist. We personally review every enquiry before sending it to you. No spam, no random enquiries. Just people who match your style and budget."

**"I need to think about it"**
→ "Of course. Take your time. I'm not going to pressure you. But if you do want to explore it, I'd suggest starting with just one or two placements — see how it goes before committing to anything."
