### Ad Creative Brief — Couples

**Visual style:** Elevated but emotionally warm. Think: editorial wedding magazine, not generic stock. Real couples, real moments, candid luxury.
**Color palette:** Champagne, ivory, sage green, dusty rose, charcoal — with gold accents
**Photography:** Natural light, outdoor/window-lit venues, close-ups of rings, florals, tablescapes. Avoid over-posed shots.
**Video:** 6–15 second vertical (9:16) clips. Real weddings. Cinematic. No talking-head unless it's a genuine couple testimonial.

**Ad formats to test:**
1. **Reels (primary):** 6–15s vertical. B-roll of beautiful weddings + text overlay hook
2. **Carousel:** 3–5 cards — "What kind of planner are you?" → personality quiz style
3. **Single image:** Hero shot + headline overlay
4. **Lead gen form creative:** Soft video or static with brand colours

**Step 1:** Build seed audiences:
- Couples: 1,000+ people who submitted lead gen form

**Step 2:** Create lookalikes:
- 1% lookalike — highest similarity, smaller volume (use for prospecting)

- Past converters
- Current customers
- Email unsubscribes

## PART 3: COMBINED STRATEGY

### Which Platform to Start First

**Start with Meta (Facebook + Instagram) first.** Here's why:
1. Lead gen forms on Meta capture both email AND qualify the lead simultaneously — Google requires a landing page
2. Visual, emotional product — weddings perform well on Instagram
3. Better audience targeting for the couple demographic
4. Lower CPC on cold traffic for the planner audience vs Google
5. Can test creative and iterate faster

**Week 4 onwards:** Layer in Google Search for planners (high-intent commercial keywords are strong there)

---

### Attribution Model

| Touchpoint | Attribution |
|---|---|
| Meta cold → Lead | Direct |
| Google cold → Landing page → Lead | Direct |
| Meta cold → Retargeted on Google → Lead | Google cross-device |
| Meta video view → Retargeted (same platform) → Lead | Meta view-through |
| Email open (if list) → Direct → Lead | Email assisted |

**Recommended model:** Data-driven attribution (Meta) + Cross-device conversion tracking (Google)

**Tracking stack:**
- Meta Pixel on all pages
- Google Tag on all pages
- UTM parameters on ALL paid links
- Zapier or Make.com to push leads into Supabase/CRM in real time
- Zapier to notify via Slack/Telegram when leads come in

### Testing Roadmap

#### Week 1: Foundation

| What | How |
|---|---|
| Launch Meta couples awareness (video) | 1 ad set, £15/day, video creative v1 |
| Launch Meta couples lead gen | 1 ad set, £15/day, lead gen form |
| Launch Meta planners lead gen | 1 ad set, £10/day, planner lead gen form |
| Install Meta Pixel on website | All events (see table above) |
| Set up Google Search couples campaign | All 50 keywords, exact + phrase |
| Set up Google Tag + conversion tracking | All conversion actions |

**Week 1 goal:** Get 5+ leads per audience. If <3, increase budget by £5/day.

#### Week 2: First Iteration

| What | How |
|---|---|
| Duplicate winning Meta creatives | 2x budget on top performer |
| Launch A/B creative variant on couples | New visual, same audience |
| Launch planner Google Search campaign | 25 keywords |
| Set up retargeting audience (website visitors 30d) | Meta Custom Audience |
| Set up lookalike audience (if 100+ leads) | 1% lookalike of converters |
| Launch Google Performance Max | Couples audience only |

**Week 2 goal:** CPL drops by 20% from Week 1 baseline. If not, swap creative.

#### Week 3: Scale & Refine

| What | How |
|---|---|
| Scale winning Meta campaigns to £25–30/day | Double down on lowest CPL |
| Pause underperforming ad sets | <50% of average CPL = pause |
| Launch lookalike prospecting | 1% + 2% tiers |
| Launch retargeting on Google Display/YouTube | Past 30-day visitors |
| Launch Meta couples retargeting (engaged) | Website visitors + video viewers |
| Review attribution — which platform is producing leads? | Google Analytics 4 + Meta Attribution |

**Week 3 goal:** 15+ qualified leads total. Identify 1 platform to scale (likely Meta).

### Retargeting Strategy

**Retargeting audience 1 — Website visitors (30-day)**
- Platform: Meta
- Creative: "Still thinking about your wedding?" + soft CTA (browse planners)
- Frequency cap: 3x per week max

**Retargeting audience 2 — Lead gen form abandoners**
- Platform: Meta
- Creative: "Your planner match is ready — complete your profile" (gentle reminder)
- Delay: 48 hours after form start

**Retargeting audience 3 — Past converters / Planners**
- Platform: Google Display
- Creative: Case study of a matched couple / planner success story

**Retargeting audience 4 — Video viewers (50%+)**
- Platform: Meta (Instagram Stories/Reels)
- Creative: Behind-the-scenes of a real Luminary wedding
- Format: Vertical video, 6–10s

**Suppression list — Exclusions:**
- Already converted (submitted lead gen or signed up)
- Unsubscribed from email list
- Current customers
